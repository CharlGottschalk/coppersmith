Templates - Partials

You might like to split your [theme]/layouts/master.html into easier to maintain parts. For this, we use partials.

When you run the copper theme command, CopperSmith will create a partials folder at [theme]/partials with a few ready made partials.

You may replace these partials with your own.

Partials aren’t required, but it helps to keep your code a little easier to maintain.

To include and render partials inside other partials or the master.html layout, you may use the handlebars partial helper.

Example
{{> nav}}

The above code will look for and include the [theme]/partials/nav.html partial.
